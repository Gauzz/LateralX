LATERALX

Experience the world of VIRTUAL REALITY and AUGMENTED REALITY.
Enjoy the stunning 360 videos.

LateralX is a Web App, where you don't have to download any application or use a specific device. Just type in the URL and you're good to go!

Products

With our Frameworks, GUI tool and Social Platform we aim to bring VR enthusiasts, amateurs, as well as new viewers together to create a bigger and stronger VR community.



LAX FRAMEWORK


In India, as a programmer grows the first language he/she is introduced to is more often c++ or java hence we have developed frameworks for these languages to help young developers create a Virtual Reality Experience without any hassle in their own familiar language.

LAX VR Creator


We are developing a GUI Toolkit based on Aframe Inspector (Open source WebVR GUI Tool) to get as many artistic people to get into VR as possible. We believe there is no power for change greater than a community, the only way to turn our vision into reality is by giving developers an easy tool to turn their imagination into reality.





SOCIAL PLATFORM


LateralX itself is a sharing platform made to let people share their VR experiences and 360 videos across the globe.
